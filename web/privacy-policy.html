Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 12, 2026

GymMate ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use GymMate.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Profile Information: Your name and fitness preferences
  • Workout Data: Exercise logs, sets, reps, weights, and workout sessions
  • Account Information: If you sign in with Google, we collect your email address, name, and Google account ID

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Features used and time spent in the app
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and browser information
  • Local Storage: Workout data stored on your device using SQLite for offline use

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email, name, and profile picture
  • Google Gemini AI: When you use AI features (photo recognition, program generation), images and descriptions may be processed by Google's Gemini API

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the GymMate service
  • Track your workout progress and personal records
  • Sync your data across devices when signed in with Google
  • Power AI features such as gym machine recognition and program generation
  • Respond to support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite. This allows GymMate to work offline and provides fast access to your workout history.

3.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you sign in with Google, your workout data is synced to our secure PostgreSQL database. This enables:

  • Cross-device synchronisation
  • Data backup and recovery

You can use GymMate without signing in — all data remains local to your device.

3.3 Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication via Google OAuth 2.0
  • Database access controls

4. Data Sharing

  • Google: For authentication (OAuth) and AI processing (Gemini API)
  • Hosting providers: Neon.tech (database) and Render.com (server) — data processed only to provide services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and update your profile in the app settings
  • Delete your local data by clearing app storage or uninstalling
  • Request deletion of cloud-synced data by contacting us
  • Revoke Google access via your Google Account settings at any time

6. Children's Privacy

GymMate is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you by posting the updated policy and updating the date above.
